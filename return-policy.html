Return & Refund Policy

We want you to be happy with your purchase. Here's how our return process works.

**Effective Date:** November 4, 2025

Our Guarantee at a Glance

You have **30 calendar days** from the date you received your item to request a return. The item must be **unused** and in the **same condition** that you received it, with the original packaging.

1. Eligibility & Conditions

Returnable Items
  • Items within 30 days of delivery.
  • Items in original condition (unwashed, unworn, unused).
  • Must include all original tags and packaging.
Non-Returnable Items
  • Gift cards or downloadable software products.
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Clearance."
  • Personalized or custom-made goods.

2. How to Initiate a Return

Follow these easy steps to start your return request:

Step 1: Login to Your Account

Access the **Order History** section and locate the order you wish to return.

Step 2: Request RMA

Select the items and the reason for the return. You will receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number.

Step 3: Ship the Item

Package the item securely, include the RMA, and mail it using the provided return label.

3. Refunds and Exchanges

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your **original method of payment**.

  • **Processing Time:** Refunds typically take **5-7 business days** to appear on your statement.
  • **Shipping Costs:** Original shipping costs are **non-refundable**.

We only replace items if they are **defective or damaged** upon arrival. If you need to exchange an item, please indicate this clearly during the online return process or contact customer support directly.

Need Help?

If you have any questions regarding your return, please visit our FAQs page or contact us directly.
