Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy details how your personal data is collected and used.

**Last Updated:** November 4, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our Services to you.

Personally Identifiable Information (PII)

  • Name, shipping, and billing addresses.
  • Email address and phone number for order communication.
  • Limited payment information (processed by secure third-party gateways).

Non-Personal and Usage Data

  • IP address, browser type, device information.
  • Pages viewed, time spent on site, and product interaction.
  • Purchase history and transactional records.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following legitimate business purposes:

  • **Order Fulfillment:** Processing and delivering your purchases, managing returns.
  • **Customer Service:** Responding to your inquiries, providing support, and sending order updates.
  • **Marketing:** Sending promotional emails and offers (only with explicit consent).
  • **Site Improvement:** Analyzing usage data to improve our product offerings and website functionality.
  • **Security:** Detecting and preventing fraudulent transactions or unauthorized access.

3. Sharing Your Information (Third Parties)

We do not sell your personal data. We only share it with trusted third parties necessary to operate our business:

**Note:** We require all third parties to respect the security of your personal data and to treat it in accordance with the law.
  • **Payment Processors:** For secure transaction processing (e.g., Stripe, PayPal).
  • **Shipping Carriers:** To deliver your purchased products (e.g., FedEx, USPS).
  • **Analytics Providers:** For monitoring and analyzing our Service usage (e.g., Google Analytics).

4. Data Security

We have implemented technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process.

We use **SSL encryption** for all data transmission and rely on **PCI-compliant** payment gateways to protect your financial data.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you have rights regarding your personal data:

Your Rights:

  • Access Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction Request that we correct any incomplete or inaccurate information.
  • Deletion Request the deletion of your personal data (Right to be Forgotten).

Your Choices:

  • Marketing Opt-out of marketing communications via the 'unsubscribe' link in our emails.
  • Cookies Adjust your browser settings to refuse all or some cookies.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies (like web beacons and pixels) to access or store information. Specific information about how we use such technologies and how you can refuse certain cookies is set out in our separate **Cookie Policy**.
